About us

We’re an employee-owned travel agency anchored by our values, integrity and dedication to customer service. We are passionate about travel and sharing the world's wonders on the leisure travel side. We organise small-group tours to get closer to nature

Explore the world

We want to offer our clients only the best that the country has to offer for such holidays: excellent kayaking, hiking beautiful nature, outstanding cuisine and friendly people. During these expeditions, you’ll get in close contact with nature and end up have quite a few new friends.

Expert guides

We have great knowledge of our tours and a passion to make your trip extraordinary. We share a passion for creating unforgettable travel experiences for our clients. We speak english and polish.

A high degree of security,

We provide travelers with peace of mind by delivering customized, safe solutions regarding transportation, accommodations and associated travel activities domestically and abroad.
